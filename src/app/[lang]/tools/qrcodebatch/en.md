# Batch QR Code Generator from Excel or CSV

This batch QR code generator creates printable QR code cards from Excel or CSV data. Upload a sheet with one content column and one name column, choose the template style, then export QR code cards in PNG, SVG, or PDF. Every QR card can automatically include the row title and sequence number, which is useful for restaurant tables, product tags, review cards, WiFi QR codes, event check-in cards, shelf labels, and offline marketing materials.

## What This Batch QR Tool Can Do

- Read Excel or CSV files directly in the browser
- Use one column as the QR code content and another column as the display name
- Batch generate QR cards with title, number, and printable layout
- Export all cards as PNG ZIP, SVG ZIP, or printable PDF
- Keep the whole workflow local without uploading the spreadsheet

## Typical Use Cases

- Restaurant table QR codes with table names and numbers
- Product tags with product title plus encoded content
- Google review QR cards for stores and counters
- WiFi QR handouts, meeting check-in cards, and venue signage
- Batch transparent or print-ready QR assets for operations teams

## How to Use It

1. Upload an `.xlsx`, `.xls`, or `.csv` file.
2. Pick the content column and the title column.
3. Choose whether to include the title and sequence number on each QR card.
4. Adjust the card size, QR size, title size, colors, and PDF page layout.
5. Export all QR cards as PNG, SVG, or PDF.

## Local Processing

Spreadsheet parsing, QR rendering, preview generation, ZIP packaging, and PDF export all happen in your browser. Data is not uploaded to a server. That makes it suitable for internal codes, store operations, inventory labels, WiFi payloads, vCard text, and other business workflows where the source file may contain private content or naming conventions.

This tool works well when you need batch QR code generation from Excel, CSV to QR code export, printable QR code labels, QR code PDF export, restaurant table QR codes, product QR cards, and other repeatable business workflows that are easier to manage from a spreadsheet.
